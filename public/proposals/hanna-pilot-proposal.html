01 / The Opportunity

Why Now?

Chronic care follow-up today relies heavily on manual phone calls and ad-hoc LINE messages. This creates three persistent constraints:

  • High nurse workload spent on routine follow-ups rather than clinical care.
  • Inconsistent patient coverage means patient status is unknown between visits.
  • Late detection of issues often happens only once patients return to the clinic with complications.

As patient volumes grow, this model does not scale. Hanna is designed to work with your existing nurses and workflows—supporting continuity of care without adding staff or disrupting delivery.

02 / What Hanna Delivers

Platform Capabilities

For Patients

  • Daily check-ins via LINE (no new app required) to maximize adoption.
  • Natural Thai voice and chat interactions, suitable for elderly patients.
  • Medication reminders and daily encouragement.
  • Simple symptom and vitals reporting outside clinic hours.

For Nurses

  • A single dashboard showing only patients who need attention.
  • Risk prioritization (Critical vs High vs Low) to guide outreach.
  • One-click call or message with full patient context.
  • Less routine chasing, more clinical focus.

For Hospital Leadership

  • Visibility into patient care between visits.
  • Clear operational metrics (engagement, adherence, interventions).
  • Evidence to support scale-up decisions based on data.
